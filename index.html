Water Sports A favorite of mine

Growing up in Saint George, UT allowed me to be exposed to water sports more than most people. Although I didn't start early, once I got on a wakeboard for the first time, it was love at first jump. I felt the waves beneath my feet and I flew. At least it felt like I was flying. It feels like you are much higher than you actually are when you hit the wake. Ever since then I have been in love with most water sports including wake surfing and slalom skiing.
